How to Play

  • Use the number buttons to enter a 5-digit guess (digits 0–9, repeats allowed). Press DEL to erase the last digit.
  • Your guess appears on the red display as you type. Press ENTER to submit.
  • After each guess, the green & yellow display reveals how close you were:
Correct digit, correct position
Correct digit, wrong position
Digit not in the code — display stays dark
  • The 6 dots below the red display track your guesses — each turns red when used, and green when you crack the code.
  • You have 6 attempts to guess the correct sequence.
